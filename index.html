YONEX Precision Racket Diagnostics

One of Indonesia’s few stores offering YONEX Precision Racket Diagnostics — a high-tech service to analyze your racquet’s performance.

This service allows you to:

  • Check racket balance & swing weight
  • Measure stiffness and torsion
  • Receive stringing or racquet recommendations based on your playing style
  • Fine-tune your gear for optimal control and power
Experience the difference of playing with a racquet perfectly tuned to your game.
